Project Description:

A fast paced game that tests your reflexes and ability to construct various objects with two word instructions. ie: Build House, etc...

Planned Gameplay:

  1. Randomly selected scenarios (from about 12)
  2. each scenario must be completed in under 5 seconds
  3. each difficulty level will reduce time available to complete scenario (level 2: 4 seconds, level 3: 3 seconds)
  4. Level 1 requires that 5 games are completed
  5. Level 2 requires that 6 games are completed
  6. Level 3 requires that 7 games are completed (this is the last level after which the game is over)
  7. High scores will be based on total time taken to finish

These are my game screen concepts.

My Production Plan

  • I am planning on doing the graphics in Pixel Art style.
  • I am planning on programming the gameplay using LibGDX (Java Framework)
  • I would like a sound engineer to assist with sounds and music.
  • Game will be playable on Mobile device and Desktop PC.
  • I have a list of Graphical Assets that someone can help me with producing.
  • I have a list of 12 Scenarios that a designer can help me with developing further.

Implemented Features

  • Basic Menu
  • Game Loop with one available Scenario ('Build House')
  • Tally Screen
